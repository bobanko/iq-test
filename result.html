How to read your report

✣ static IQ - your iq calculated only by correct answers, no correlation with others who passed the test

🏆 global rank - this means your place in global ranking, based on correct answers

🔝 top rank - your place in %

🧠 scientific IQ - a local estimate based on mean and standard deviation from the current sample

⏱️ completion time - how much time you spent to complete test

❓ questions answered - how many answers you made

✅ correct answers - how many were correct

📅 date taken - date when you passed (the test)

🎯 accuracy rate - correct answers compared to total answer count

🏎️ answer speed - how much time you spent per question (avg)
